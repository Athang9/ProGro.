10-26-26

This grade is high in potassium and phosphorus and low in nitrogen, which is good for main application since autumn. It is particularly valuable for crops that require large amounts of available phosphorus and potassium in the soil.
It is highly efficient on soils with low potassium content, fine-textured soils with a leaching water condition.


Used for:-
Used as a complex fertilizer for supply of all three major nutrient like Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potash.
Used in basal application in crops like Wheat, Paddy, Maize, Pulses, Sugarcane, Vegetables etc.

Usage Instructions :-
It should be applied during sowing and through broadcasting.
