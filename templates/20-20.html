20 20 Garden Fertilizer has a balanced ratio of nitrogen, phosphorous and potassium that works great for fertilizing plants at all stages of growth. This water soluble formula dissolves easily in water and can be applied by pouring alongside plants or with an injection system.
This is a water-soluble formulation that dissolves easily, even in cold water. Use it to fertilize seedlings prior to transplanting, or to feed established plants in the vegetable garden.
It can be mixed in a 5 gallon bucket and administered by pouring alongside plants, or used with injection systems like our Brass Siphon Mixer and Hoss Fertilizer Injector.

Used for:-
Hydroponics/hanging gardens also performs very well for flowering plants and fruit-bearing plants.
