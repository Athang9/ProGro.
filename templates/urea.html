UREA

Urea is a source of Nitrogen, an essential nutrient crucial for crop growth and development.
Urea is the most important nitrogenous fertilizer in the country because of its high N content (46%N)
How to Use Urea - - Since Urea is highly Nitrogen-concentrated, it should be used in combination with earth or sand before its application.
It should not come in contact with the seeds. It also can be applied as a top dressing.

Used for:-
Kitchen gardens, Terrace gardens, Pot/Container plants, Indoor plants, Ornamental plants, Flowering plants, Outdoor plants, Lawns, Planting beds, Organic farming, Agriculture etc.

Usage Instructions :-
Used 1 - 2 teaspoon per flower pot or Gardening plants at a frequency of 1/2 weeks.
